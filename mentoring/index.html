How do we get a participant to return?

It's important that participants return because...

We can do that by...

  • make sure people feel welcome
  • match tasks to participants, ensure they are successful the first time
  • keep people engaged even when they struggle
  • help without being overbearing
  • enable folks to continue afterward on their own
  • say hi in irc

In public

pre turning them, expose everyone to bits of the mentoring process:
mentor in public, in a public channel in irc, or in a room where people can overhear interactions between mentors and participants. seeing how mentors mentor is like training future mentors

Let others see how mentors...

  • Make sure people feel welcome
  • Match tasks to participants
  • Keep people engaged even when they struggle
  • Help without being overbearing
  • Enable folks to continue afterward on their own

answer questions you don’t know the answer to

  • say I dont know
  • lets find out together
  • this is how I would find out, start by...
  • I would ask …
They feel like they dont know everything. Let them see people doing mentoring who also do not know anything.

Tips(new title)

  • continue to be a resource
  • be available for questions
  • participate side by side
  • letting new people make mistakes (10 second rule)
  • offering specific suggestions to improve
